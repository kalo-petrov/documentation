## What are Actions?

**Actions** let you interact with your tracking platform's API beyond just pulling stats. Where the Stats page is read-only reporting, Actions let you actually **do things** — create offers, edit conversions, enable affiliates, update campaigns, and more.

Everything happens inside AffZero. You select a connection, choose what you want to do, fill in the parameters, and AffZero makes the API call to your tracker on your behalf and shows you the result.

---

## Who Should Use Actions?

Actions are designed for network managers and operations teams who regularly perform tasks like:

- Creating new offers in bulk across multiple connections
- Editing conversion statuses after fraud review
- Enabling affiliates on offers without logging into the tracker
- Updating campaign costs or campaign routing in Binom
- Generating affiliate tracking links programmatically

You don't need to be a developer — the forms guide you through each required field with clear labels.

---

## How to Use Actions

1. Go to **Actions** in the left sidebar
2. Select a **Connection** from the dropdown (your Affise or Binom connection)
3. The available actions appear, grouped and color-coded by method:
   - **Get** (blue) — read data
   - **Add** (green) — create new records
   - **Edit** (amber) — update existing records
4. Click any action to expand its form
5. Fill in the required fields and click **Execute**
6. Results appear immediately below the form

---

## Actions vs. Tracker Action Steps

Actions in this section are **manual, on-demand** operations — you run them when you need them, directly from the Actions page.

For **automated** tracker operations that run on a schedule, use the [Tracker Action steps](/automations/steps/tracker-actions) in the automation builder. The two are complementary:

- Use manual Actions for day-to-day operations (creating offers, editing conversions)
- Use Tracker Action steps to automate recurring operations (e.g. automatically update costs every week)
