## Connections

A **connection** is a saved API credential for one external tracking platform. Each live platform account counts as one connection.

- The **Demo connection** does not count toward the limit
- Connections can be reused across all your automations
- Deleting a connection frees up the slot

## Automations

Any saved automation — enabled or disabled — counts toward the limit. Deleting an automation frees up the slot.

## Steps Executed

Each time an automation runs, every step in its workflow counts toward the period-based steps-executed limit. The counter resets at the start of each billing period.

## Gmail SMTP Accounts

The number of Gmail accounts you can connect simultaneously. All accounts are available as "Send From" options in any Send Email step.

## Forwarding Addresses

Forwarding addresses (e.g. `auto-7gk2p9xr@inbox.affzero.com`) used by the **Email Received** trigger. Each address can be shared across multiple automations. Manage them in **Providers → Email Forwarding**.

| Plan | Addresses |
|---|---|
| Free | 0 |
| Starter | 1 |
| Pro | 5 |
| Scale | 10 |

## AI Calls

Consumed by:
- AI Analyze step (1 call per run)
- AI Extract step (1 call per run)
- Generate Invoice Number step (1 call per run)
- Describe It (1 call per automation generated)
- Generate with AI buttons in email/invoice editor (1 call per use)
- AffZero AI chatbot (1 call per message)

Calls reset at the start of each billing period.

## Upgrading or Downgrading

Go to **Settings → Billing** to change your plan at any time.

- **Upgrades** take effect immediately — new limits apply right away
- **Downgrades** take effect at the end of the current billing period

<Warning>
  If you downgrade and your current usage exceeds the new plan's limits (e.g. you have 30 connections on Pro and downgrade to Starter which allows 10), existing resources are **not deleted** — but automations may fail to run until you bring your usage within the new limits.
</Warning>

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Can I have multiple users on one account?**
AffZero currently operates on a single-user-per-account model. Team access is on the roadmap.

**Do unused AI calls roll over to the next period?**
No — AI calls reset at the start of each billing period and do not accumulate.

**What happens if I hit my automation limit?**
You can still edit existing automations, but you won't be able to create new ones until you delete an existing one or upgrade your plan.

**Is there an annual discount?**
Contact us for annual billing options.
