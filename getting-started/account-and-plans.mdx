## Plan Overview

AffZero offers four plans designed to grow with your team. Limits apply per organization.

| Feature | Starter | Pro | Pro+ | Business |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Price** | $19/mo | $49/mo | $129/mo | $269/mo |
| **Connections** | 1 | 3 | 8 | 10 |
| **Automations** | 3 | 10 | 30 | 100 |
| **Schedules** | Monthly only | Weekly + Monthly | Daily + Weekly + Monthly | All |
| **AI Calls / month** | 20 | 100 | 500 | Unlimited |

<Note>
  All plans include access to the visual automation builder, the Stats page, email templates, invoice templates, and Runs & Logs.
</Note>

## What counts as a Connection?

A connection is a saved API credential for one tracking platform account. If you have two separate Affise networks (e.g., one for brand A and one for brand B), that counts as two connections.

The Demo connection does **not** count against your connection limit.

## What counts as an Automation?

Any saved workflow in the Automations section — whether enabled or disabled — counts toward your limit. Deleting an automation frees up the slot.

## What counts as an AI Call?

Each time the AI performs a task, it consumes one AI call:

- **AI Analyze step** runs → 1 call
- **Generate Invoice Number step** runs → 1 call
- **Generate with AI** button used in email/invoice editor → 1 call
- **AffZero AI chatbot** message sent → 1 call

AI calls reset on the first of each month.

## Schedule Availability by Plan

Not all schedules are available on every plan:

| Schedule | Starter | Pro | Pro+ | Business |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Run Once | ✓ | ✓ | ✓ | ✓ |
| Monthly | ✓ | ✓ | ✓ | ✓ |
| Weekly | — | ✓ | ✓ | ✓ |
| Daily | — | — | ✓ | ✓ |

<Warning>
  If you downgrade your plan, automations with schedules not available on the new plan will be automatically disabled. You can re-enable them after upgrading again.
</Warning>

## Viewing Your Usage

Go to **Settings → Usage** to see a real-time breakdown of:

- How many connections and automations you've used vs. your limit
- AI calls consumed this month vs. your allowance
- Your current plan and billing cycle

## Upgrading or Downgrading

Go to **Settings → Billing** to change your plan at any time. Upgrades take effect immediately. Downgrades take effect at the end of your current billing period.
