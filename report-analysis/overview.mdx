## What is Report Analysis?

**Report Analysis** is a standalone tool that lets you combine data from multiple sources, transform it with AI assistance, and produce a clean output report — without writing formulas or code.

You describe what you want in plain English. AffZero's AI builds a step-by-step transformation plan. You review the plan and click **Run**. The result can be downloaded as a CSV or written directly to a new tab in your Google Sheet.

---

## When to use it

- Merge two or more exported CSV reports that don't share the same format
- Filter, group, and sort affiliate data to create a custom summary
- Cross-reference subaccount IDs or sub-IDs across multiple platform exports
- Produce a clean report from raw tracker exports without touching a spreadsheet

---

## How it works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Add your sources">
    Upload up to 3 CSV files, or select tabs from a connected Google Sheet. AffZero reads the column headers and a few sample rows — it does not send your full data to the AI.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Write an instruction">
    Describe what transformation you want in plain English. For example: *"Keep only rows where Sub ID 2 is in the list [partner_a, partner_b], then group by affiliate name and sum revenue, sorted by revenue descending."*
  </Step>
  <Step title="Generate a plan">
    Click **Generate Plan**. AffZero sends the column names and sample rows to the AI, which returns a structured transformation plan. You can review every step of the plan before running it.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Run the plan">
    Click **Run Plan**. The transformation is executed locally against your data — no raw rows are sent to the AI. Results appear instantly.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Export or save to Google Sheets">
    Download the result as a CSV, or write it to a new tab in one of your connected Google Sheets.
  </Step>
</Steps>

---

## History

Every successful run is saved to your **History** panel (last 25 runs). Click any past run to see:

- The original instruction you wrote
- The plan that was generated
- The full result table
- A link to the Google Sheet, if you wrote the results there

You can also click **Reuse for new report** to open a fresh workspace pre-filled with the same instruction and plan — useful when you receive new weekly exports and want to run the same transformation on fresh files.
