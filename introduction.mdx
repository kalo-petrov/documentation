## What is AffZero?

AffZero connects to your affiliate tracking platforms (like Affise, Binom, or CAKE) and lets you automate the repetitive work your team does every week: pulling stats, generating affiliate invoices, and sending performance emails.

Instead of logging into your tracker, exporting a spreadsheet, copying numbers into an email, and sending it to each affiliate one by one — you set up an automation once and AffZero does it on a schedule.

## Who is AffZero for?

AffZero is built for **performance marketing managers and affiliate network teams** who:

- Manage a roster of affiliates and send them regular stats or payment emails
- Need to generate invoices based on live payout data
- Want to automate repetitive reporting without building internal tools
- Use tracking platforms like Affise, CAKE, or Binom

You don't need to be technical to use AffZero. If you understand your tracking platform and can navigate its reporting section, you can build a full automation in minutes.

## How it works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Add a Connection">
    Connect your tracking platform (Affise, CAKE, Binom, or the built-in Demo) by entering your API credentials. This is a one-time setup.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Build an Automation">
    Use the visual automation builder to chain together steps: pull stats → check conditions → send email. Each step passes data to the next.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Schedule It">
    Set the automation to run daily, weekly, or monthly — or trigger it manually at any time with Run Now.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review Runs">
    The Runs & Logs page shows every execution, step-by-step results, and any errors so you always know what happened.
  </Step>
</Steps>
