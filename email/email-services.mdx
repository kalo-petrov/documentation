## Overview

AffZero needs an email service configured before it can send any emails. You have three options, each suited to different scales and technical comfort levels.

Go to **Providers → Email** to set up or manage your email services.

---

## Choosing the Right Service

| | Gmail SMTP | Resend | SendGrid |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Setup difficulty** | Easy | Easy | Easy |
| **Volume** | Low (Gmail sending limits apply) | Medium–High | High |
| **Best for** | Small teams, getting started | Growing teams | Enterprise, high volume |
| **Cost** | Free (uses your Gmail) | Free tier, then paid | Free tier (100/day), then paid |
| **Tracking** | No | Open/click tracking | Open/click tracking |
| **Accounts per plan** | 1–3 Gmail accounts | 1 | 1 |

## Gmail SMTP

Gmail SMTP sends emails directly from one of your Gmail addresses using Google's mail servers. No external service required.

### Setup

1. Go to **Providers → Gmail & Drive**
2. Click **Connect Gmail Account**
3. Sign in with your Google account and grant permission
4. Your Gmail address is now available as a "Send From" account in Send Email steps

### Notes

- You can connect up to **3 Gmail accounts** depending on your plan (Starter: 1, Pro+: up to 3)
- Gmail has daily sending limits (~500 emails/day for regular Gmail, 2,000/day for Google Workspace accounts)
- Emails are sent from your actual Gmail address — recipients see your real address in the From field

<Warning>
  AffZero encrypts your Gmail credentials at rest using AES-256-GCM encryption. However, if you change your Google password or revoke app access, you'll need to reconnect the account.
</Warning>

## Resend

[Resend](https://resend.com) is a developer-friendly email API designed for transactional emails. It offers a free tier and excellent deliverability.

### Setup

1. Create an account at [resend.com](https://resend.com)
2. In Resend, go to **API Keys** and create a new API key
3. Verify your sending domain in Resend (add the DNS records Resend provides to your domain)
4. In AffZero, go to **Providers → Email** and select **Resend**
5. Paste your Resend API key and enter the "From" address (must use your verified domain)
6. Click **Save**

### Notes

- Domain verification is required — you can't send from a Gmail address via Resend; you need a custom domain (e.g. `noreply@yourcompany.com`)
- Resend's free tier allows 3,000 emails/month and 100/day
- Paid plans start at $20/month for higher volumes

## SendGrid

[SendGrid](https://sendgrid.com) by Twilio is an enterprise email platform with strong deliverability, detailed analytics, and high sending limits.

### Setup

1. Create an account at [sendgrid.com](https://sendgrid.com)
2. In SendGrid, go to **Settings → API Keys** and create a key with **Mail Send** permissions
3. Verify your sending domain in SendGrid (Sender Authentication)
4. In AffZero, go to **Providers → Email** and select **SendGrid**
5. Paste your SendGrid API key and enter the "From" address (must use your verified sender)
6. Click **Save**

### Notes

- SendGrid's free tier includes 100 emails/day
- Paid plans scale to millions of emails/month
- SendGrid provides open rate and click tracking, unsubscribe management, and detailed delivery logs

## Using Multiple Email Accounts

If you have multiple Gmail SMTP accounts connected, the **Send From** dropdown in the Send Email step shows all of them. You can send different automations from different addresses — for example, one address for affiliate communications, another for billing.

Resend and SendGrid each support one configuration at a time.

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Email not delivered — Gmail SMTP">
    Check that your Google account hasn't revoked AffZero's access. Go to your Google Account → Security → Third-party apps and verify AffZero is listed. Also check Gmail's daily sending limits.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Email not delivered — Resend or SendGrid">
    Verify that your sending domain's DNS records are correctly configured and verified in Resend/SendGrid. Check the API key has the correct permissions. Look at the delivery logs in your Resend or SendGrid dashboard for bounce or rejection reasons.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Emails landing in spam">
    This is usually a domain reputation issue. Make sure you've completed domain verification (SPF, DKIM, DMARC records) in your email service. Avoid spam trigger words in subject lines.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
